Can we attend your defense? What should we expect

Yes of course! If you happen to be in the Boston area, my defense will be on MIT campus, in Building 33 room 206. For MIT Aero Astro, PhD defense start with a 1 hour presentation that I will give on my topic. Then there will be an open question period for people to ask questinos (yes you!), and then there will be a series of more technical questions from my committee. After the question period (which can last up to an hour), the committee convenes together in private to determine if I get my PhD. This too, lasts an undefined amount of time. After they have made their determination, they bring me out and announce whether I got the PhD.
